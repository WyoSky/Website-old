The Economic Engine for the Space Industry

We Curate and Develop Space

Our Mission

WyoSky is facilitating the collision of the space industry and blockchain technologies. The organization is the first space based DAO LLC registered in Wyoming with a tokenized governance managed by council members and token owners. The organization's primary mission is to function as a space governance to effectively facilitate the economic engine for the growth of the space industry.

Democratizing Access to Digital Space Assets (DSA)

Where We All Go, We Go As One

Our economic model enables participants to access space investments through our decentralized financial tooling. The digital asset securty, development, and compliance councils discern the available funding opportunities to contribute to the Digital Space Asset ecosystem. These Digital Space Assets will encompass many sectors within the space industry from infrastructure, communications, satellites, data, launch, and tourism.

Innovating Incentive Networks and Economic Models

Frictionless and Borderless Space Currencies

We have created a programmable currency - Photon - with taxable transactions and a monetary policy managed by the DAO. The monetary policy determines the distribution from the taxed transactions, such as burn events, reflection, and delegation to proposals. Photon also functions as a yield reward token which is payed out to participants staking their Digital Space Assets (DSA)
